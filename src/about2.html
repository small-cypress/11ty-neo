about

I'm a cis person in my late 30's. I work in the visual arts professionally - not tech - and I like antiquated printing methods. I own two printing presses. Union employee. I like to build and fix things.

Interests: cemeteries, local history, animal husbandry & aquariums,

Politically: a leftist who doesn't purity test beyond reason. I'm into good faith interactions on the 1:1 scale to change minds. I think more cis white people could take up this mantle since it's not possible for so many people. I worked in poor rural Southern hospitals and have seen what the intentional destruction of public education, decreasing quality of life, and decades of propaganda have done to people. I know 1:1 good faith conversations can work because they pulled me out of some pretty alarming views, and I have the bandwidth and ability to talk to "problematic" people if they want to talk to me. I feel very strongly that we need a path for people to come back. I don't think people are as bad as the worst thing they've done.

Aspiring: baker, birthday-rememberer, runner, neighbor-knower

Former: dinghy sailor, higher ed lecturer, Floridian
