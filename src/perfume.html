indie fragrances

Most people are very surprised that I care about fragrances at all - I don't seem like the "type". I got really into them during the pandemic and early sobriety. My sense of smell was essentially fried from alcohol, so as it came back it opened new worlds. I think I was probably too online and ended up in the r/indiemakeupandmore subreddit somehow (???) and hearing about the absolute magic people were ordering in the mail.

Indie fragrance oils aren't expensive and you can order small samples of different scents for around $3USD at Ajevie, or check out r/indiemakeupandmore's Sunday post to buy samples or full size perfumes second-hand. There are a lot of people who purchase hundreds of bottles - which seems excessive but maybe that's their thing - and are happy to destash. Sellers have spreadsheets of perfumes that they use in those posts, and it (and pixel whoever's) post in 32-bit Cafe made me feel like I should keep a log.

I have taken a break from actively collecting while my sinuses recover, so all of these were purchased 6 months or longer ago.
