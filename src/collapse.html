Since the pandemic I have thought a bit about collapse in a really non-attached way. I don't believe any one person's theories of climate collapse/peak oil/end-stage technofeudalism/etc, but I do listen to a wide range of voices on the topic.

I like hearing about action items and ways to support my nervous system in the turbulence we are currently in. Will it lead to collapse? I don't know, but I like to fill out the scarier parts of the "what if?" flow chart without getting too doomy.
